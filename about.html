Interesting Facts About Chameleon

  • Chameleon is a reptile that is part of a iguana suborder
  • Changing skin colour is an important part of communication among chameleons

Interesting Facts About Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts About Camel

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Interesting Facts About Cicada

  1. Cicadas have five eyes
  2. Cicadas can be black, brown or green and can have red, white or blue eyes

Interesting Facts About Panda

  • Pandas have great camouflage for their environment
  • They spend a lot of their day eating

Interesting Facts About Teddy-Bear

  1. A teddy bear is a stuffed toy in the form of a bear
  2. The incident of Roosevelt sparing the bear led to the popularity and naming of Teddy bears.
